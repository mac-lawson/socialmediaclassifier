About

Welcome to the Pixelizer RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) platform! For our final project at Tufts, we are training a machine learning model to classify images. From there, the images are run through a sentiment analysis model and given a tag detirmining if they are safe to post on social media or upload to an image hositng site. By helping us train datasets for the image classification model, you are imroving the effectiveness of our scanning engine!
